## BACS 3001 Capture The Flag Experience

As part of the BACS 3001 course, I participated in a Capture the Flag (CTF) competition that provided hands-on experience with real-world cybersecurity concepts. The challenges required critical thinking, research, troubleshooting, and the practical application of technical skills.

Throughout the competition, I used Linux-based tools, web application testing techniques, network analysis tools, and command-line utilities to investigate and solve challenges. The experience reinforced the importance of persistence, attention to detail, and systematic problem solving.

## Key Lessons Learned

One of the most valuable lessons I learned during the CTF was the importance of methodical troubleshooting. Many challenges required testing multiple approaches, researching unfamiliar concepts, and learning from unsuccessful attempts before arriving at a solution.

The experience also demonstrated the value of maintaining detailed notes and documenting findings throughout an investigation. These practices help improve efficiency, reduce duplicate work, and support clear communication of technical findings.
